Our Story

Frederick and Stephanie

Stephanie and Frederick met at Northwestern University as undergraduates in Mathematics. After a short year together, they were separated by distance with Stephanie going to graduate school at Purdue followed by Frederick going to UCLA. However, they tried to see each other as often as possible, often times through cross-country red-eye flights. Now they are happily together in the same city, San Francisco, and can't wait to get married at Frederick's childhood home in Merritt Island!
